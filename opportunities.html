Why Join SSTL?

SSTL offers a collaborative research environment with strong mentorship, impactful projects, and excellent institutional support.

Research Environment

  • Work on transportation & climate change, MaaS, discrete choice modeling, AI, and data science.
  • Collaborative culture with opportunities to publish and present internationally.
  • Access to data-driven methods (agent-based simulation, ML, econometrics, optimization).

Social Benefits & Support

  • Postdocs as ULaval employees can access social benefits.
  • Scholarship opportunities and funding pathways are available for graduate and postdoc candidates.
  • Safe, affordable, high-quality living environment in Québec City (UNESCO heritage city).

Ready to Apply?

Email your application to transport@gmc.ulaval.ca. Include your CV, research interests, and relevant documents.
