Postdoctoral Research Fellow (Industrial Engineering)

About

Xuelin Tian is a Postdoctoral Research Fellow at the Smart & Sustainable Transportation Lab (SSTL). She is the recipient of the IVADO Postdoctoral Scholarship. Her work focuses on evaluating transportation measures and their impacts on greenhouse gas (GHG) emissions.

Awards

  • IVADO Postdoctoral Scholarship — Recipient

Research Interests

  • Transportation decarbonization
  • Policy evaluation and impact assessment
  • GHG emissions modeling
  • Urban energy systems
  • Data-driven mobility analysis

Projects

  • Evaluate the performance and impact of different measures on GHG emissions
