Meet our Principal Investigator

Dr. Bobin Wang leads the Smart & Sustainable Transportation Lab with over 10 years of research experience in mobility analysis, transportation planning, and the application of AI and data science to solve complex urban challenges. Her work focuses on creating more efficient, equitable, and environmentally friendly transportation systems.
