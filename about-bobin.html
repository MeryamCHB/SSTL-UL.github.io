Prof. Bobin Wang

Dr. Bobin Wang

Assistant Professor

Department of Mechanical Engineering

Principal Investigator

Department of Mechanical Engineering, Université Laval.

Bobin Wang, Ph.D., is a professor in the Department of Mechanical and Industrial Engineering at Université Laval. She holds a Ph.D. in transportation engineering and has over 10 years of research experience in mobility analysis. Her work covers transportation planning, environmental studies, smart cities, and intelligent transportation systems.

She has research experience across China, Europe, and North America, with more than 50 publications and 2 monographs. She is the principal investigator of several national and provincial projects in Canada supported by NSERC and FRQNT. Her current and future research focuses on climate change, smart cities, and intelligent transportation systems.
